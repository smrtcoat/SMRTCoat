Market Analysis

The flexibility of SMRT Coat allows for a wide-spread permeation through every level of sport.

23.6 million Americans are involved in athletics, 57% in contact sports

Current solutions are generally electrical or mechanical accelerometers which are attached to the exterior of an athlete’s helmet. There are some prototypes which reside on the helmet’s interior however none of these devices are currently available for purchase.

These devices also lack effectiveness. In many cases, as reported by former pros such as Daniel Carcillo, trainers or coaches will ignore the potential threat of injury in favour of keeping players in the game for a better overall performance. This is seen time and time again in professional sports leagues like the NHL and NFL and puts athletes at major risk of repeated trauma and worsening of head injuries.
