SMRT Coat

Colour Changing Coating for Concussion Detection

Problem Statement

There are three million concussions per year as reported by the University of Pittsburgh Medical Centre.

  • 50% of concussions go unreported or undetected

Concussive symptoms are insidious and long-term:

  • Memory Loss, dizziness
  • Post-concussive syndrome (PCS)
  • Chronic Traumatic Encephalopathy (CTE)

Our Solution

SMRT Coat: a spray-on helmet coating containing encapsulated dye nanoparticles.
